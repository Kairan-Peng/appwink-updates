AppWink 0.1.22 Release Notes

AppWink v0.1.22

This release tightens the Hidden Bar user-facing contract after real menu-bar Command-drag testing showed that the old two-symbol marker-to-arrow interval could overstate the area actually controlled by AppKit's pusher status item. It keeps existing hotkey bindings, license state, update settings, and Toolbox preferences compatible with 0.1.21.
